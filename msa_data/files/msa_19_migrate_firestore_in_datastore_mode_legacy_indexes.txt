Customer message:

Hello Firestore customer,

You are receiving this communication because one or more of your projects use legacy composite index definitions in Datastore mode.

Legacy index definitions will no longer be honored after December 31, 2026. Queries that depend on them will begin returning errors.

What you need to know

Key change: Legacy composite index definitions stop being honored on December 31, 2026.

Impacted customers/accounts:

Your affected projects are listed below:

${project.project_id}

What you need to do

Action required before December 31, 2026:

Export your current index definitions: Use the gcloud CLI to list indexes in the affected projects.
Re-declare indexes: Convert your definitions to the current index.yaml format and deploy them.
Validate queries: Confirm that your query paths resolve against the newly built indexes before removing the old definitions.

We're here to help

Review the index configuration documentation for the current format.

If you have any questions or need help, contact Google Cloud Support.
